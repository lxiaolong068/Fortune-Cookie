## The Science of Positive Affirmations and AI

Positive affirmations have research behind them. The foundation is **self-affirmation theory**, developed by psychologist Claude Steele in the 1980s. It argues that affirming core values buffers stress by reinforcing self-worth. Later fMRI work at UCLA showed that repeating affirmations activates the brain's reward centers (the ventromedial prefrontal cortex) while reducing activity in the amygdala's fear response. Practically: lower reactivity, steadier mood.
