## DIY Gift Idea: The "Jar of Destiny"

Skip the Amazon gift card. This year, give your grad something they'll actually remember.

![A colorful jar that can be used for notes and messages](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1649701954731-e9bfba805c78?crop=entropy&cs=tinysrgb&fit=max&fm=jpg&ixid=M3w4NTEwNzB8MHwxfHNlYXJjaHwxfHxjb2xvcmZ1bCUyMGdsYXNzJTIwamFyJTIwbm90ZXMlMjBwYXBlcnxlbnwwfHx8fDE3NzA0OTU2OTZ8MA&ixlib=rb-4.1.0&q=80&w=1080)

### What You Need

- A mason jar or any clear container with a lid
- Colored paper strips (cut to ~1" × 4")
- A pen (or a printer, if you're fancy)
- Ribbon or stickers for decoration
- 20-30 fortune cookie messages from this list (or [generate custom ones](/ai-generator))

### How to Make It

1. **Pick your messages**: Choose 20-30 fortunes from this page. Mix funny, inspiring, and major-specific ones.
2. **Write or print** each message on a separate paper strip.
3. **Fold each strip** and place it in the jar.
4. **Decorate** the jar with ribbon, a label ("Jar of Destiny"), or stickers.
5. **Add a note** that says: *"When life gets confusing, pull a fortune."*

<Callout type="tip" title="Why This Beats a Gift Card">
The Jar of Destiny works because it's personal (you chose the messages), interactive (they open a new one whenever they need it), and lasting (it sits on their desk for months). It costs under $5 and takes 20 minutes. That's a better ROI than any gift card.
</Callout>

## FAQ

### What do you write in a graduation fortune cookie?

A graduation fortune cookie message works best when it's **short, forward-looking, and slightly fate-like**. Combine encouragement with a playful "prediction" tone. Example: *"Your next chapter starts now."* Avoid long quotes or generic "congratulations" lines—the fortune cookie format rewards brevity and surprise.

### What are funny fortune cookie messages for graduates?

The best funny graduation fortunes are **self-aware and relatable**, not mean-spirited. They acknowledge the absurdity of suddenly being an "adult." Examples: *"Student loans build character. Allegedly."* or *"Your diploma is valid even if your sleep schedule isn't."* Match the humor to Gen Z sensibility: dry, honest, a little self-deprecating.

### How do you make fortune cookies for a graduation party?

You have two options: **physical** or **digital**. For physical cookies, order custom fortune cookies from a specialty bakery and provide your messages (use our list above).
