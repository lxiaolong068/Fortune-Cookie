## What Makes a Birthday Fortune Cookie Message “Work”?

Good fortune-cookie-style birthday lines usually have three traits:

- **Short** (one to two sentences; easy to print on slips)
- **Positive** (but not overly cheesy)
- **A tiny twist** (a wink of “fate” or playful wisdom)

If you’re printing these as party favors, aim for **under ~90 characters** when possible.

## FAQ

### What do you write in a birthday fortune cookie?

A birthday fortune cookie message works best when it’s **short, upbeat, and slightly “fate-like.”** One sentence is ideal; two is fine if it stays punchy.

### How long should a fortune cookie message be?

For printable slips, aim for **8–14 words** or roughly **50–90 characters** so it doesn’t wrap awkwardly.
