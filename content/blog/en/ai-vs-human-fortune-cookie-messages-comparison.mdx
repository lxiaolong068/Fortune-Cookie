## What Makes a Great Fortune Cookie Message?

Before we dive into the battles, let's define what elevates a fortune cookie message from meh to memorable. At its core, a stellar fortune nails a few key traits:

### Brevity with Punch
Fortunes max out at 7-10 words—short enough to fit on a tiny slip, punchy enough to stick. No rambling; every syllable counts.

### Universality Meets Surprise
It should resonate broadly yet twist unexpectedly. Think "A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor"—profound, applicable to anyone, with a fresh angle.

### Emotional Spark
Whether inspirational, humorous, or mystical, it evokes feeling: hope, laughter, reflection. Cultural nods add authenticity without clichés.

### Open-Ended Wisdom
Great fortunes invite interpretation, sparking "aha!" moments. They're not predictions but prompts for self-discovery.

Human writers draw from lived experience, idioms, and intuition. **AI**, fueled by vast datasets, mimics patterns but sometimes misses nuance.
