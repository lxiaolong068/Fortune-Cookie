When organizing a baby shower, the host often struggles to find favors that are affordable, memorable, and not destined for the trash can. Fortune cookies check every box:

1. **The Joy of Discovery**: There's an undeniable thrill in breaking open a fortune cookie. It turns a standard party favor into a tiny, interactive experience.
2. **Highly Customizable**: Because the message is hidden inside, you can tailor it perfectly to the parents' personalities—be it sarcastic, deeply emotional, or tailored to an inside joke.
3. **Budget-Friendly**: Custom fortune cookies are relatively inexpensive, making them a great option for guest lists of any size.
4. **Theme Flexibility**: Whether your baby shower theme is "A Grand Adventure," "Twinkle, Twinkle," or just a classic pastel gathering, fortune cookies fit right in.

## Creative Ways to Use Fortune Cookies at a Baby Shower

You don't just have to hand them out in a goodie bag as guests leave. Here are some fun ways to incorporate your baby shower fortune cookie messages into the party itself:

### 1. The Gender Reveal Fortune
If the parents are revealing the gender at the shower, consider handing out specialized pink- or blue-tinted fortune cookies. Guests break them open simultaneously to read the enclosed message: "It's a Boy!" or "It's a Girl!"

### 2. The Advice Jar Alternative
Instead of having guests write advice in a guestbook, provide them with blank strips of paper to write their own "parenting fortune." You can later compile these or even bake them into homemade cookies if you're feeling ambitious!

### 3. The Interactive Shower Game
Hide a "winning" fortune inside one of the cookies. For example, 29 cookies might say "You are a great friend," but one says, "You win the centerpiece!"
